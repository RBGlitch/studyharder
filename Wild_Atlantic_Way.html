Wild Atlantic Way

The Wild Atlantic Way is a tourism travel on the west coast, and on parts of the north and south coasts, of Ireland. It is 1600 miles (2600 km) in length, is one of the longest defined coastal routes in the world. It runs along the entire east coast of Ireland from the Inishowen Peninsula in the north to the picturesque town of Kinsale, County Cork, in the south. This route reveals from start to finish the wonders of nature, the power of the ocean and its influence on the west coast of Ireland, as well as the stunning countryside in all its diversity. During the trip, you can go on an excursion to the island of Skellig Michael. This is the place where the 7th episode of “Star Wars” was filmed
